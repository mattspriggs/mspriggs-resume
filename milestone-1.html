Resume Website for Matt Spriggs

Purpose, Audience and Goal

Purpose

This website will assist Matt in making a successful career change from other industries to the Information Technology field, specifically Fullstack Web Development after completing the bootcamp and moving to New Zealand

Audience

There are two primary target audiences for this website (NOTE:connectivity to LinkedIn and to be able to give out the URL easily for networking is essential for all audiences):

  • Employers
    • New Zealand based companies
      • Established technology companies that provide services to other businesses, namely website development and maintenance for other companies
      • Companies that have internalized their web development, be it internally used web based systems or companies that provide
    • Remote employers
      • Established technology companies that provide services to other businesses, namely website development and maintenance for other companies
      • Companies that have internalized their web development, be it internally used web based systems or companies that provide
  • Freelance Work
    • Work given out by companies via contract
    • This work is more likely to be received through contacts at companies

Goal

The goal is to find a way to make income post an overseas move while breaking into a new industry. Additionally, it will serve as a platform to exhibit a growing portfolio of work to lay the foundation for creating a freelance and website development business.
